Empowering Youth

My services are focused on supporting children, adolescents and their families facing overwhelming emotions, difficult transitions and interpersonal difficulties. Specific conditions that are known to respond well to CBT & ACT psychotherapy are anxiety and excessive worrying, low mood, impulsivity, anger outbursts and social difficulties.

Depending on the needs of your family, frequency of the sessions and duration of treatment can vary. Sometimes just a couple of sessions can make a notable difference, providing youth with skills to take a different look at the situation or to apply strategies we explore and practice in session.

One way to see this process is an analogy with arranging stones in a new balanced pattern or finding a path in a new landscape.
