Services by Dr. Anna

Individual psychotherapy and parent consultations are available by virtual and in-person sessions. In-person sessions and over secure video to all residents of Ontario, Canada.

In-person appointments are held on an as-needed basis in Waterloo, Ontario.
What to expect:

In our free 15 minute check & connect phone call with a caregiver I will ask you about your hopes and concerns for the therapy, and provide you with a brief overview of the process.

You will have a chance to ask questions and we can discuss the best plan of action for you and your child. Depending on the age of the child and the goals, the first session might be with just parents/caregivers, parents/caregivers and youth, or just youth.

Often, the initial check & connect phone call is a start of the therapy process - a first step in identifying goals for the therapy and establishing what a good plan of action might look like for your family.

Connecting with Heritage

My part-time practice is located in UpTown Waterloo on land that was granted to the Haudenosaunee of the Six Nations of the Grand River, and is within the territory of the Neutral, Anishinaabe, and Haudenosaunee peoples.

As a settler I continue to learn the traditions of this beautiful land. As a professional, I respect and cherish traditions and beliefs that every family brings with them.

As a youth therapist, I support youth in building their skills in self-awareness and self-reflection, while providing them with a safe non-judgmental space.
