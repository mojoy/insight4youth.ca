What to expect:

To start, we will set up a free 15 minute check & connect phone call with a caregiver. During the call the goals and desired outcomes, as well as concerns will be discussed, and I will provide a brief overview of the next steps.

You will have a chance to ask questions and we can discuss the best plan of action for you and your child. Depending on the age of the child and the goals, the first session might be with just parents/caregivers, parents/caregivers and youth, or just youth.

Often, the initial check & connect phone call is a start of the therapy process - a first step in identifying goals for the therapy and establishing what a good plan of action might look like for your family.

Acknowledgement

My part-time practice is located in UpTown Waterloo on land that was granted to the Haudenosaunee of the Six Nations of the Grand River and is within the territory of the Neutral, Anishinaabe, and Haudenosaunee peoples.

As a settler, I continue to learn the traditions of this beautiful land. As a professional, I respect and cherish traditions and beliefs that every family brings with them. Stories, beliefs, rituals, and customs that your family maintains and passes on to children can foster a sense of belonging and connectedness.
