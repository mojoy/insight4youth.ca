My Approach: Every Client and Every Family is Unique

My approach to therapy is always centred on compassionate client-centred care grounded in evidence-based research.

While working in collaboration with my clients I focus on their specific strengths, goals and needs, while also considering their unique store circumstances.

With over a decade of research and clinical work in hospitals, schools and community clinics, I am passionate about providing best care to young people facing difficulties.

Psychotherapy is a form of treatment that allows young people and their families to become more aware and more skilled in coping with difficult situations and overwhelming feelings.
